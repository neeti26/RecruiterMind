Senior Machine Learning Engineer — AI Platform Team

About the Role:
We're looking for a Senior ML Engineer to join our AI Platform team. You'll be building the infrastructure and models that power our recommendation and personalization systems at scale. This isn't a research role — we need someone who can take models from prototype to production and keep them running reliably.

What You'll Do:
- Design, train, and deploy large-scale ML models (recommendation, ranking, NLP)
- Build and maintain ML pipelines using Airflow, Spark, and Kubernetes
- Collaborate with data scientists to productionize their models
- Improve model monitoring, A/B testing frameworks, and feature stores
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical roadmap

What We're Looking For:
Required:
- 5+ years of experience in machine learning engineering or data science
- Strong Python skills — you write clean, testable, production-grade code
- Experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow for model training
- Hands-on experience with MLOps: model serving, monitoring, CI/CD for ML
- Experience with distributed computing (Spark, Ray, or Dask)
- Solid understanding of SQL and data warehousing concepts
- Experience deploying models on cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with LLMs, RAG systems, or vector databases
- Contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience with Kubernetes and Docker for ML workloads
- Knowledge of recommendation systems or ranking algorithms
- Publications or blog posts on ML topics

The Ideal Candidate:
You've shipped ML models to production before and know the pain points. You care about reliability and scalability, not just model accuracy. You can communicate technical decisions to non-technical stakeholders. You're comfortable with ambiguity and can drive projects independently.

Team Culture:
- Fast-moving, high-ownership environment
- Strong engineering culture — code reviews, testing, documentation matter
- Remote-friendly with async-first communication
- We value learning and give time for exploration
